About Company

PT ITO SEISAKUSHO ARMADA

PT Ito Seisakusho Armada is a manufacturing company specializing in progressive dies, stamping parts, pressed components, and precision manufacturing technology for automotive and industrial applications in Indonesia.

Located in Bekasi, West Java, the company combines Japanese manufacturing standards with local engineering expertise to deliver accurate, reliable, and high-quality products. Through continuous improvement, disciplined production processes, and strict quality control, PT Ito Seisakusho Armada supports the needs of automotive component manufacturers and industrial customers.

The company is supported by advanced machinery, CAD/CAM systems, skilled engineers, and experienced production teams. With a strong focus on precision, productivity, and customer satisfaction, PT Ito Seisakusho Armada continues to contribute to the growth of the manufacturing industry in Indonesia.
